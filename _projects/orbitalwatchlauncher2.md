OWL2 (Orbital Watch Launcher 2) is a 1-bit missile-defense arcade game for Playdate focused on
tight feedback loops, legible visuals, and satisfying chain reactions.

## Responsibilities

- Designed the core loop around scarce ammo and bonus shots
- Implemented gameplay and UI in Lua with a custom level system
- Iterated heavily based on live playtest sessions

- Strong emphasis on clarity on the Playdateâ€™s small screen
- Inverted-color mode for outdoor visibility

- The importance of testing on real hardware rather than just the simulator
